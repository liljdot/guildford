Welcome!
You’ve taken the first step to benefitting from all the incredible health benefits regular walking in a group brings.

With the support of our trained walk leaders, you will enjoy safe walking routes at a pace that works for you.

You will soon feel healthier while exploring your local area, and make new friends

Your next step is to complete our Walker Registration form – please print it (see the form above), complete it, and bring it along to your first walk with us. It’s also useful for us to know who your “In Case of Emergency” contact is – see the ICE form above – print it, complete it, and carry this in your pocket. Add your “In Case of Emergency” contact as ICE on your mobile phone.

When you complete our walker registration form, you sign a data protection statement. This means that we will respect your privacy and will never sell or share your data without your permission. The information you provide to us helps us communicate with you – to notify you should there be a walk cancellation – and to send you walking-related information.

If you have any questions, please contact our Membership Secretary.
Email: Membership@guildfordhealthwalks.org.uk

Please share your walking experiences on social media – tag us: @GuildfordHealthWalks.
