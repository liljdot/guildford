Free Guided Short Walks




Every weekday. All abilities welcome


Walking makes you happy and keeps you healthy.

Join one of our walk groups. It will give you the chance to safely explore the outdoors, get to know your local area and meet new people. We have a morning health walk for every weekday. All our walks are led by trained volunteer walk leaders.

Regular walking has many benefits:

  • Improved mental health
  • Helps prevent diabetes
  • Helps to reduce weight
  • Boosts your immune system
  • Prevents development of osteoporosis and osteoarthritis in certain groups
  • Improved muscle strength, and functioning of heart and lungs
  • Helps reduce blood pressure
  • Can have a significant impact on the risks associated with two of the most common forms of cancer....
  • You make new friends!

About Guildford Health Walks

Guildford Health Walks started life in 2009. The first group joined a national Health Walks community, initially under the auspices of Natural England, later" managed by the Ramblers. Over the years that first walk group has increased to five, with walks on offer around Guildford and the villages, including Worplesdon, Shalford, and Shere. In 2015 we became a Community Group Association run by a committee. We are currently 35 trained volunteer walk leaders taking turns to lead around 20 to 25 FREE walks a month. All of our walks offer the option of refreshments after the walk, ideal for getting to know the other walkers better.
