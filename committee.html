Here you will find all the documents used by the Guildford Health Walks managing committee

Guildford Health Walks is a community group association run by volunteers. The management committee consists of a chairperson, secretary, treasurer, support members and walk group coordinators.

Committee meetings take place in February, May, and September. The AGM is held in November.

“

I want to take this opportunity to thank all our committee members for the splendid work done ensuring the smooth running of our health walks.

”

Annelize Kidd, Walks Organiser

Guildford, United Kingdom
