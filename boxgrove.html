Boxgrove Walk Group


Mondays

Meet at 9.45 am for 10 am start next to Peacocks Greengrocer, Boxgrove shops, Cunningham Avenue, GU1 2PF.
Take bus 23, or park along the neighbouring roads.

Email: Boxgrove@guildfordhealthwalks.org.uk

Length: 60 minutes to 90 minutes

First Monday of the month: Explore Riverside Park
Second Monday: Visit Merrow Downs
Third Monday: Beautiful views from the top of Pewley Down
Last Monday: Amble around Stoke Park

All the walks have some inclines. Some can be muddy in winter.
Optional refreshments afterward at Boxgrove Park coffee shop.
