Boxgrove Walk Group


Mondays Shorter Walk

Meet at 10.15 am at the bottom of the ramp in front of Spectrum Leisure Centre for walks less than an hour long.
(Wait for the walk group to arrive from the Boxgrove shops.)

First Monday of the month: Explore Riverside Park (50 minutes). Can be muddy in winter;
Last Monday: Amble around Stoke Park (45 minutes). Suitable for wheelchair and disability scooter users. Easy, mostly level walks.
Optional refreshments afterward at Spectrum Leisure Centre.
